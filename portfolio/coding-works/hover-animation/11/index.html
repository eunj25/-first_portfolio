• Jay Park

    Jay Park is the first-ever Asian artist to sign with JAY-Z’s label, ‘Roc Nation’, proving himself as a global artist. His U.S. debut EP [ASK BOUT ME] and a collaboration album [This Wasn’t Supposed To Happen] with Hit-Boy received attention from fans all over the world and successfully finished his first world tour ‘JAY PARK 2019 SEXY 4EVA WORLD TOUR’ of 18 countries and 31 cities in total.

  • Simon Dominic

    Simon Dominic is one of the iconic figures in the Korean Hip-Hop scene who is beloved by many. From his beginning as the hottest underground rookie to a CEO, and now returned as an artist, he has been not only dominating the Korean Hip-Hop scene but also has become an irreplaceable figure in the overall entertainment and media industry as well.

  • Loco

    Loco has been widely loved for his sincere lyrics and melodic flow by both domestic and international fans. Ever since winning the reality rap competition TV show, Show Me the Money Season 1, he has dropped multiple platinum hits including ‘Hold Me Tight (Feat. Crush)’, ‘You Don’t Know’, ‘Too Much (Feat. DEAN), It Takes Time (Feat. Colde)’ and ‘It’s been a while (Feat. Zion.T)’

  • Woo

    Before his debut, Woo appeared in the rap competition TV show Show Me the Money 6, which led to his breakout debut single ‘We Are (Feat. Loco, GRAY)’ that topped all mainstream music charts in Korea for weeks. The artist signed with AOMG in 2017 and since then he released the single [Anxiety] and EP [af] and collaborated with fellow artists from the label in a single [BALANCE] featuring Loco produced by CODE KUNST.

  • CODE KUNST

    CODE KUNST’s unparalleled ability to produce smooth and genre-bending beats satisfies a diverse range of audiences including Hip-Hop fans. His music captures everyday sounds better than anyone else’s, allowing the listeners to recognize his music right away.
